Got a Style Question?

From custom orders to fabric choices, we’ve answered your most frequent fashion concerns. Let’s keep you informed, glam, and confident!

Can I customize my outfit to suit a specific event?

Absolutely! We specialize in custom creations. Just share your event type, inspiration, and preferred fabric — and we’ll design something magical for you.

What’s the average timeline for completing an outfit?

Typically, custom orders take 7–14 working days depending on complexity. Rush orders may attract additional charges but are always delivered with our signature finesse.

Do you handle bridal wear or Aso Ebi group orders?

Yes! Whether you’re a bride or organizing Aso Ebi for a squad, we create stunning coordinated looks that turn heads. Bulk pricing and fittings are also available.

Can I visit your studio for fittings?

Definitely! We encourage in-person fittings for perfect measurements. Kindly book an appointment in advance via our contact form or social handles.

Do you ship outside Nigeria?

Yes, we ship worldwide! We work with reliable courier partners to ensure your fashion gets to you — wherever you are.

What’s your return or adjustment policy?

As every piece is tailor-made, returns are rare. However, we offer free minor adjustments within 3 days of delivery to ensure you feel absolutely fabulous.
